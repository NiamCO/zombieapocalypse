EMERGENCY SURVIVAL KIT

Your emergency kit is your first line of defense against the NIAMVIRUS. Ensure you have all these items prepared and accessible at all times.

Protective Gloves & Mask

Gloves and a mask can prevent the mucus from touching you. Use thick, durable materials that the virus cannot penetrate. Replace after each potential exposure.

Food & Water Supply

Store at least 2 weeks of non-perishable food and 1 gallon of water per person per day. Include high-energy foods and water purification tablets.

Non-Lethal Weapon

A weapon for defense, with non-lethal options preferred in case a cure is created. Consider tasers, pepper spray, or blunt objects to immobilize without killing.

First-Aid & Medicine

Comprehensive first-aid kit including bandages, antiseptics, pain relievers, and any personal prescription medications. Include antiviral supplies if available.

Insulated Protective Jacket

A thick, insulated jacket to completely eliminate any chance of the virus entering the body through skin contact. Should be waterproof and tear-resistant.

Shelter on High Ground

Secure shelter on high ground or with strong walls. The infected have difficulty with elevation and fortified positions provide critical safety during outbreaks.

How Infection Occurs

People contract NIAMVIRUS by playing video games for over 12 hours continuously, or through direct contact with infected individuals. The virus attacks the brain, destroying neurons until it takes control of cognitive functions.

How NIAMVIRUS Affects the Body

1. Immune Response

Pathogens from infected mucus bypass barriers, triggering immune response. Macrophages engulf pathogens while T-cells signal B-cells to respond.

2. Cellular Warfare

B-cells and T-cells multiply to fight the invasion. Some become memory cells that don't respond to the first invasion, leaving the body vulnerable.

3. Neural Destruction

The virus travels to the brain, tricking brain cells into destroying themselves. This neural degradation happens rapidly once the blood-brain barrier is crossed.
